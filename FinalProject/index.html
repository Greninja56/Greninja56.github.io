Thomas Parameswaran

The name’s Thomas, and I like to consider myself an entertainer! Entertainment comes from a lot of things for me — sports, games, stories — all things I’ve woven my own touch into.

I’ve had the amazing experience of playing soccer for nearly my entire life, just under 20 years now. I’ve been lucky enough to represent my club teams, my high school team, and even my college club team, competing both at home and in other states.

I wouldn’t say COVID was one of my favorite times, but it did bring me one of my most-loved activities: writing! Primarily feel-good fiction, I write stories about my favorite created duo and their adventures, the thrill of a high-seas pirate, and characters whose sole purpose is to break the fourth wall. I enjoy using themes of loyalty, perseverance, loss, and rebirth to define my stories and myself.
